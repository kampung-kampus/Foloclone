About GUI, Kampung Kampus located next to Khatib Hometeam

At Kampung Kampus, our mission is to close the loop of food and soil through community-powered composting. We believe in regenerating our land and producing nutrient-rich compost, all while fostering a sustainable lifestyle.

Composting with us means embracing nature’s cycle, minimizing food waste, and nourishing our earth for generations to come.

Visit Kampung Kampus

Why Compost?

Enriches Soil

Boosts soil health, fertility, and structure without chemical fertilizers.

Reduces Waste

Diverts food scraps from landfills, lowering methane emissions.

Supports Sustainability

Promotes recycling and composting of food waste through local initiatives.

How It Works

1. Collect

Gather food scraps from homes & businesses.

2. Deliver

Bring organic waste to the composting site.

3. Compost

Transform scraps into rich compost through natural processes.

4. Nourish

Return nutrients to farms and gardens for healthy crops.
